💡 Smart Savings Tips

✅One big grocery run on Day 0 — bulk buying saves ~30% vs daily shopping.
✅Skip Jungfraujoch — adds 600+ CHF for the group, exhausting for kids/elderly, and your plan already has better value.
✅Swiss tap water is excellent everywhere — refill bottles instead of buying.
✅Fuel up near Interlaken — cheaper than mountain towns. Fill the tank once every 2 days.
✅Parking: always use paid lots (fines in Switzerland are brutal — ~100 CHF). Budget is already included.
✅Day 3 chill option saves ~300–400 CHF vs Grindelwald. Consider it if energy is low after Lauterbrunnen!

🌤️ Late April / May Weather Tips

🌡️Valley temp: 10–18°C. Mountain tops: 0–5°C. Layers are essential.
❄️Grindelwald First / Harder Kulm tops: still cold, possibly snow. Warm jacket for mountain excursions.
🌧️Spring showers possible. Pack a light waterproof jacket for everyone (foldable ones = ideal).
💧Waterfalls at MAXIMUM flow in May — snow melt at its best. Perfect timing!
👟Comfortable walking shoes for everyone, including elderly. Some paths can be slightly muddy.
🕶️Sunscreen + sunglasses — mountain UV is much stronger than at sea level!

Yash Chopra & the Swiss Alps

Yash Chopra filmed over a dozen movies in Switzerland across four decades — so many that Swiss Tourism credited him with single-handedly making the Alps a dream destination for Indian travellers. Interlaken, Grindelwald, and the Berner Oberland were essentially his open-air studio. You're travelling in his footsteps. 💛
